# Unterschied T-SQL vs PostgreSQL

- T-SQL: IDENTITY
- PostgreSQL: SERIAL

- T-SQL: GETDATE()
- PostgreSQL: NOW()

- T-SQL: TOP 10
- PostgreSQL: LIMIT 10

## Cheat Sheet

| Funktion        | T-SQL      | PostgreSQL |
|----------------|-----------|-----------|
| Auto Increment | IDENTITY  | SERIAL    |
| Date           | GETDATE() | NOW()     |
| Top Rows       | TOP 10    | LIMIT 10  |
